Who We Are

About G&A KCash Microfinance

G&A KCash Microfinance Inc. A Christian Individual aims to build a community founded on shared commitment, mutual investment and a christ centered where faith, hope and love reflect to lives of men and women.

Our Mission

To develop communities with initiative combining economic empowerment with spiritual growth offering small loans, individual savings and financial education to help individuals escape poverty.

Our Vision

To be a leading financial individual who provides excellent service to poor people in the community to create benefits for clients, shareholders and society. As well as exploring to the great commission in christ. serve.
