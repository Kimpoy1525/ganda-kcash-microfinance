Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

G&A KCash Microfinance Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our loan application services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when filling out our loan application form, including: full name, contact number, email address, complete address, desired loan amount, purpose of loan, and preferred branch.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to process your loan application, communicate with you regarding your application status, improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), strict Content Security Policy, input sanitization to prevent XSS attacks, and rate limiting to prevent abuse. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with regulatory bodies as required by applicable law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at kcashmicrofinanceheadoffice@gmail.com.

Who We Are

About G&A KCash Microfinance

G&A KCash Microfinance Inc. — A Christian individual aiming to build a community founded on shared commitment, mutual investment, and a Christ-centered life where faith, hope, and love reflect in the lives of men and women.

Our Mission

To develop communities with initiative combining economic empowerment with spiritual growth, offering small loans, individual savings, and financial education to help individuals escape poverty.

Our Vision

To be a leading financial institution that provides excellent service to people in the community, creating benefits for clients, shareholders, and society — while exploring the Great Commission in Christ.

What We Stand For
